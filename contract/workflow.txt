1 steps for create a NFT
  - nft.mintNFT("ipfs://example-token-uri");  -> mint the nft -> returns - tokenId
  - nft.approve(address(marketplace), tokenId);  -> approval of NFT to marketplace
  - marketplace.listNFT(address(nftContract), tokenId, price));

2 steps for reList a NFT
  - nft.relistPurchasedNFT(oldListingId, newPrice);
  - nft.approve(address(marketplace), tokenId);  -> approval of NFT to marketplace
  - marketplace.listNFT(address(nftContract), tokenId, price));
